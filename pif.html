Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Pay It Forward promotion?
The Pay It Forward promotion allows Patriot Mobile members to prepay for a full year of service and receive two additional months for free. Here’s how it works: Prepay for 12 months of service and you will receive a credit equal to the amount you paid forward (“PayForward Credit”) PLUS another credit equal to two months of service at your current plan charges (“2 Months FREE Credit”).
How do I take advantage of the Pay It Forward promotion?
You can participate in the promotion by logging into the MyAccount portal, navigating to the Pay It Forward tab, and prepaying for a year of service. The 2 Months FREE Credit will automatically be added to your account.
Can I use the Pay It Forward promotion more than once?
Yes! If you've already used the promotion, you can do it again to lock in your current rate and get another 14 months’ worth of service, at your current plan charges.
What happens if I upgrade my plan after prepaying for a year?
If you upgrade your plan or add a line during the 12-month period, you will consume your PayForward Credit balance before the 12-month period and will be responsible for the incremental charges, after the 2 Months FREE Credit is fully applied.
Is the credit applied to my entire account or just one line of service?
You can prepay for the entire account or individual lines of service. In the event a customer prepays by line of service, the Credits’ balances will be applied to the service charges on the entire account rather than to the respective line of service. Therefore, if you do not prepay on all lines of service on an account, the PayForward Credit balance will run out before the 12-month period as the Credit will be applied to actual charges on the entire account. Once the PayForward Credit balance is fully applied to your service charges, then the 2 Months FREE Credit balance will be applied to service charges until fully used.
What if I cancel my account before using all 14 months of service?
If you leave before the 12 months, you will only receive a refund of your remaining PayForward Credit balance which will be used first to apply to any outstanding recurring service charges. The value of the 2 Months FREE Credit will not be refunded.
Can I combine the Pay It Forward promotion with other offers or discounts?
Yes, the Pay It Forward promotion can be combined with most other discounts, promotions, or offers.
Can I apply the Pay It Forward promotion to multiple lines on my account?
Yes, you can prepay for multiple lines, and the free months will apply to the overall account. The credit will be distributed across all lines based on your account balance.
