Frequently Asked Questions

What is Patriot Mobile Connect?
Patriot Mobile Connect offers wireless internet access using specific hotspots (Patriot Puck) and router (Patriot Router) devices.
How do I choose between the Patriot Router and Patriot Puck?
Your choice depends on the number of devices you plan to connect and how you plan on connecting them. The Router supports 32 devices and requires an A/C power outlet, while the Puck supports 10 devices and has 16 hours of charged battery life.
See device pricing and product comparison.
What data plans are available with Patriot Mobile Connect?
We offer a range of data plans starting from 2GB to 1000GB.
What network towers do these devices receive signals from?
The device's vSIM capabilities allow for seemless connections to a cellular tower with the strongest signal from either AT&T, T-Mobile, and US-Cellular.
Can I connect devices via a wired connection?
The Patriot Router includes four Ethernet ports for wired connections. The Patriot Puck only connects to devices wirelessly.
Is there a contract requirement for the devices?
No contract is required when purchasing a device or any of the data plans. Occasionally, specific promotional giveaways may require a commitment.
Can I change my data plan later?
Data plans can downgraded or upgraded at any time during the month. Call (972) PATRIOT to make any plan changes.
What are the speed capabilities of the devices?
Both devices offer competitive download and upload speeds suitable for various online activities, from browsing to streaming. See FAQ 3 for device specifications.
Is there an early termination fee?
An early termination fee may apply if the device was purchased during a promotional offer.
