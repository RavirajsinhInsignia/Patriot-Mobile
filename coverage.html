What is a Coverage Guarantee?

Patriot Mobile is one of the only mobile carriers offering wireless coverage on all three major networks.

Because our service uses the same towers as ALL THREE of the big carriers, if you’re ever experiencing service issues, we can switch you for FREE to the service most appropriate for your area.

Call 972-PATRIOT. One of our US-based member service agents will get you switched to the right network coverage.

4G/5G capable devices are required for 4G/5G service, respectively. Cellular service is not available in all areas and is subject to system limitations. Coverage in areas outside of the continental U.S. is specifically excluded from the Coverage Guarantee.
